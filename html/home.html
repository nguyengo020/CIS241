Hi everyone! My name is Ngoc Nguyen, but I usually go by An or Anne. This is my first year at college. I was born in Vietnam and just moved to America 4 years ago. My family ended up here at Lynnwood, Washington because one of my cousins used to study here at Edmonds College and he recommended us. It is one of the biggest changes that my family ever made in the whole life. The life here at United States is way different than what life is like in Vietnam. But luckily that we are able to engage and integrate to the lifestyle here.

Let me tell you the story of how I end up here studying about web development. One day, I received an email from the school district that offered junior students an opportunity to apply to work at T-Mobile. On the last day of the due date, I felt bored and had nothing to do. So, I just took out my computer and quickly write out a resume to submit. My thought at that time is that it is just for fun and I did not expect to get it since I have no experience at all and little knowledge about technology. After one month passed by, I finally got an email that said congratulations. At that time, I was suprised with what I see and immediately accept the offer from T-Mobile because it is one of the greatest opportunity that everyone wants to have. I am a person that hates to decide and choose what my life is going to be in future. I only care what is currently happen to my life right now. So, I just stick with it until now.

Family is really important to me. My mom and dad divorced and are separated when I was young. So, I usually stay at home with my mom whenever I have spare time outside of school and work. I have three older brothers. Two of them live here with me and my mom. Only one of them is living out of state in Minnesota. Whenever there is a national holiday, we mostly gather back together either in Minnesota or Washington and celebrate the holiday. Or sometimes, we will meet in a certain state and travel together. I really appreciated what I have now because I believe that not all people get what they want in life.
